Written Instructions


CAMEL

Interesting Facts About Camel

1. There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.

2. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.

3. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.


CHAMELEON

Interesting Facts About CHAMELEON

1. THEIR FEET WORK LIKE SALAD TONGS.

2. ALMOST HALF OF ALL KNOWN SPECIES LIVE IN MADAGASCAR.

3. CHAMELEONS VARY WILDLY IN TERMS OF SIZE.

4. THEY MAINLY CHANGE COLOR IN ORDER TO COMMUNICATE OR REGULATE BODY TEMPERATURE.

5. SKIN CRYSTALS ENABLE THEM TO CHANGE COLOR AT WILL.


CICADA

Interesting Facts About Cicada

1. Cicadas are not locusts.

2. Cicadas have one of the longest insect lifespans.

3. Cicadas inundate forests as a survival mechanism.

4. Humans eat them, too.

5. Their lengthy life cycles may help them evade predators.


PANDA

Interesting Facts About Panda

1. They have great camouflage for their environment.

2. Their eyes are different to normal bears.

3. Cubs are well protected in their first month.

4. Courageous cubs!

5. A helping hand.

6. They spend a lot of their day eating.

7. Bamboo is critical to their diet.

8. But they do occasionally eat something other than bamboo.


PIGEON

Interesting Facts About Pigeon

1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.

2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.

3. Pigeons are highly sociable animals.

4. Pigeons mate for life, and tend to raise two chicks at the same time.

5. Both female and male pigeons share responsibility of caring for and raising young.
